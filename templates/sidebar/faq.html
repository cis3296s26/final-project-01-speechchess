From the sidebar, choose one of the play modes:

  • Play Now — A local two player game played on the same device.
  • Play AI — Playing the computer/AI with the option of Easy, Medium, and Hard mode.
  • Play a Friend — Create or join a room and play online with a friend.
  • Play Online — A Ranked matchmaking against other players. Your rank (Elo) will be affected.

You do not need an account with Play Now or Play AI mode. An account is required for online play to track your ELO rating. Sign up with the Sign-Up button in the sidebar. Enter an email address and a password. After the Account created successfully! message appears, proceed to log in with your email and password.

You must say Speech Chess first so the app knows the next words are a chess command, not general speech.

Examples:

  • Speech Chess e2 to e4 — move a piece (from → to).
  • Speech Chess highlight e2 — highlight legal moves from a square.
  • Speech Chess undo — undo the last move.
  • Then say confirm or submit move to execute a pending move.

ELO is a number that represents your skill level relative to other players — think of it like a ranking. The higher your ELO, the better you are. Winning a Play Online match will increase your ELO and losing/forfeiting will decrease it.

Beating a higher rated opponent gains you more points and losing to a lower rated opponent costs more points.

Your current ELO is shown in the sidebar next to your profile and on your profile page.
